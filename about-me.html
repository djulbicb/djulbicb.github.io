I am a Java Developer with a background in 3D Design and Architecture.

Software development is my passion but that is not something I was always aware of. I studied architecture and worked for a couple of years in the field of arch visualisation and 3D in general.

As I advanced, I got into scripting in order to automate time-consuming tasks. This drew me further into software development. Through programming, I found the missing pieces I had been searching for in architecture. To further enhance my skills, I pursued courses and self-taught studies, continuously expanding my expertise in software development.

I am dedicated to creating structures that seamlessly combine functionality, modularity, and sustainability. My goal is to become a software project architect. To achieve this, I thrive in diverse environments that challenge me to grow both technically and professionally.

My primary focus lies in Java and React, however I'm adaptable and always eager to learn new technologies.
